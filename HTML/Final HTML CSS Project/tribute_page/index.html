Markiplier

Markiplier, born Mark Edward Fischbach on June 28, 1989, is a popular American YouTuber known for his energetic Let's Play videos, especially in the horror genre with games like Five Nights at Freddy’s. Since launching his channel in 2012, he has built a massive following thanks to his expressive personality and deep voice. Beyond gaming, he’s known for creative projects like the interactive series A Heist with Markiplier and In Space with Markiplier, as well as the one-year-only channel Unus Annus. He also co-hosts the podcast Distractible and is recognized for raising millions for charity through livestreams.
